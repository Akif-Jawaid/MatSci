About us

Welcome to MaterSci Express (MSE) , a distinguished journal committed to the prompt dissemination of top-tier research within the materials science domain

Our mission is to furnish a robust platform for scientists, scholars, and researchers to showcase their cutting-edge discoveries and advancements.

MaterSci Express (MSE) stands as an interdisciplinary, peer-reviewed journal that serves as a nexus for research endeavors spanning across the realms of science, engineering, and medicine, offering a comprehensive reference outlet

MSE facilitates the dissemination of original research articles, reviews, and communications pertinent to significant scientific and technological breakthroughs, uniting the expertise of materials scientists, chemists, physicists, biologists, engineers, ceramicists, metallurgists, theoreticians, and technocrats. Embracing both fundamental and applied research, MSE is dedicated to exploring the latest frontiers of advanced materials.

Our journal covers a wide array of topics within the field of materials science, including:

> Metals and Ceramics

>Alloys (e.g., high-entropy alloys, shape memory alloys)

>Non-crystalline Materials and Glasses

> Composite Materials

> Dental and Bio Materials

> Medical applications of materials

> Materials in Extreme Conditions

> Photovoltaic Devices and Materials (e.g., perovskite, organic,& silicon solar cells)

> Artificial Photosynthesis Materials

>Energy Harvesting Technologies and Materials (including nanogenerators)

> Thermoelectric Devices and Materials

>Materials for Batteries and Supercapacitors

>Flow Batteries and Related Electrode/Electrolyte Materials

>Fuel Cell Technology and Related Materials

>Catalytic Materials and Processes for Energy Generation, Conversion, and Storage

>Nanocomposites

>2D Materials and Coatings

>Nanomaterial and Nanodevice Applications in Various Fields

>Quantum Materials and Devices

>Information Device Physics and Engineering

>Smart Sensing Devices and Systems

>Materials Theory, Prediction, Design, and Genome

>Applications of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Materials Science

>Interatomic Potentials/Force Fields

 

Readership:

Our journal attracts a diverse readership comprising professionals and researchers from various disciplines, including materials science, chemistry, engineering, information science, electronics, physics, biology, energy science, environmental science, medicine, and pharmaceutical science, both from academia and industry.
